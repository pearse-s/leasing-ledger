Leases

Property · Unit Tenants Status Type From End Rent Balance Agent
Every current lease (plus the most recent lease on vacant units), mirrored daily from Buildium with balances, deposit state, attribution, and renewal status. Click a lease for tenant contacts, recurring transactions, and the live ledger — itemized charges & payments pulled straight from Buildium the moment you open it (never stored). Balance = the outstanding-balances mirror for active leases, or money received to date on future leases (“pre”). Sidebar scope filters apply.

Key manager

Property · Unit Bin Status Holder Out Due back Last activity
Migrated from the Monday Key Ledger — this is now the system of record. Click a key to check it out / in, change status, or add a note; every action is logged in History. Sidebar scope filters apply to matched keys. Every action is logged under the name you picked on the sign-in screen — sign out and back in to change it.

Tasks

Property · Unit Task Type Status Priority Due Assignee
Live from Buildium — maintenance requests, resident requests, and to-dos. Defaults to open (new / in progress); use the chips for overdue, maintenance, unassigned, etc. Sidebar scope filters apply. Click a row for full detail, the update history, and any linked work order.
